📊 Superstore Sales Analytics & Forecasting Dashboard


🚀 Overview

Power BI dashboard to analyze retail sales performance and forecast short-term trends.


Focus: clear insights → better business decisions

🎯 Objective
Identify top-performing products and regions
Analyze sales, profit, and customer segments
Understand shipping and payment behavior
Forecast future sales (15-day prediction)


📈 Key Metrics
Sales: 2M
Profit: 175K
Quantity: 22K
📊 Dashboard Highlights

1. Sales Performance

  West region leads (33%), followed by East (29%)

  Corporate segment contributes the most (33%)

  COD is the most used payment method (43%)
